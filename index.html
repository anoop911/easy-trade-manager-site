Intelligent Automation & Discreet Trading

Smart Sizing & Risk Control

Easily define your position size and risk before execution.

  • Enter the Risked Amount or Lot size or % of Balance (New!)
  • **Automatic lot** size calculation for precise risk management.
  • **Trade with fixed Amount** or fixed Lot Size based on your strategy.

Discreet Order Execution (Stealth Mode)

Keep your trading intentions hidden from your broker until the moment of execution.

  • All orders are hidden (you will not see it until it is executed!).
  • All SL/TP levels are hidden from the broker, ensuring broker visibility only after execution.
  • **Hidden features** for scalping with Magic Trail.

Intuitive Setup on Chart

Use visual lines on the chart for quick setup and dynamic adjustment.

  • Drag the **RED line** on the chart to fix the Stop-Loss.
  • Drag **Blue line (New!)** for Limit/Stop orders automatically!
  • Adjust TP1, TP2 and TP3 lines easily.
  • **Buy/Sell** > Adjust the lines even after the trade - New!
